proof of uniqueness of Lagrange Interpolation formula


Existence is clear from the construction, the uniqueness is proved by assuming there are two different polynomials p⁢(x) and q⁢(x) that interpolate the points. Then r⁢(x)=p⁢(x)-q⁢(x) has n zeros, x1,…,xn and there is a point xe such that r⁢(xe)≠0. r⁢(x) is non-constant with degree deg⁡(r⁢(x))≤n-1 and has more than n-1 solutions, which is a contradiction. Thus there can only be one polynomial.
