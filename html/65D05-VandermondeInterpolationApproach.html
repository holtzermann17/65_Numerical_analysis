Vandermonde interpolation approach


The Vandermonde approach for interpolation is when we wish to determine the interpolating polynomial p⁢(x)=a0+a1⁢x+a2⁢x2+…+an⁢xn for the n+1 points (xi,yi), i=0,1,…,n by forming the equations yi=a0+a1⁢xi+a2⁢x22+…+an⁢xnn for i=0,1,…,n, and solving for the unknown coefficients a0,a1,…,an.

The system of equations can be written by using matrices Y=X⁢A where X is a Vandermonde matrix.
